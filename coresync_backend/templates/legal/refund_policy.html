Refund Policy

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

1. Overview

At CoreSync, we strive for complete customer satisfaction. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds across our various services and products.

2. Membership Refunds

2.1 Monthly Memberships

  • No refunds for partial months
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period
  • Members retain access through the paid period
  • No refunds for unused services within a billing period

2.2 First-Time Members

  • 30-day satisfaction guarantee for new members
  • Full refund if cancelled within 30 days of first payment
  • Applies only to first-time memberships
  • Must not have used more than 2 services

2.3 Annual Memberships

  • Pro-rated refund available within first 60 days
  • After 60 days, no refunds for early cancellation
  • Discount rate acknowledged when calculating refunds

3. Service Booking Refunds

3.1 Cancellation Timeline

  • 24+ hours before: Full refund
  • 12-24 hours before: 50% refund
  • Less than 12 hours: No refund
  • No-show: Full charge, no refund

3.2 Service Issues

If you're unsatisfied with a service, please notify us immediately. We will:

  • Offer to redo the service at no charge
  • Provide a credit for future services
  • Issue a full refund if neither option is satisfactory

4. Shop Product Refunds

4.1 Return Conditions

  • Products must be unopened and unused
  • Original packaging must be intact
  • Returns accepted within 14 days of pickup
  • Proof of purchase required

4.2 Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened skincare products (hygiene reasons)
  • Used supplements or consumables
  • Final sale or clearance items
  • Custom or personalized items

4.3 Refund Process

Approved returns are refunded to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

5. Concierge Service Refunds

5.1 Request Cancellation

  • Before approval: Full refund of any deposits
  • After approval, before processing: 90% refund (10% processing fee)
  • During processing: 50% refund
  • Ready for pickup: No refund (item is sourced)

5.2 Declined Requests

If we cannot fulfill your concierge request, any deposits or payments will be fully refunded.

6. Refund Processing

6.1 Timeline

  • Refund requests processed within 2 business days
  • Stripe refunds appear in 5-10 business days
  • Bank processing times may vary

6.2 Method

  • All refunds issued to original payment method
  • Cannot split refunds across multiple payment methods
  • Store credit available as alternative option

7. Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny refunds in cases of:

  • Violation of facility rules
  • Fraudulent activity or chargebacks
  • Abuse of refund policy
  • Damage to property or equipment

8. Gift Certificates

  • Non-refundable and non-exchangeable for cash
  • Valid for 12 months from purchase date
  • Can be transferred to another person
  • Cannot be combined with other promotions

9. Disputes

If you have concerns about a charge or refund, please contact us first. We are committed to resolving issues fairly and promptly. If we cannot reach a resolution, the matter will be handled according to the dispute resolution process outlined in our Terms of Service.

10. Contact for Refunds

To request a refund or discuss concerns, contact us at:

  • Email: refunds@coresync.life
  • Phone: +1 (551) 574-2281
  • Address: 1544 71st Street, Brooklyn, NY 11228
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST

11. Policy Changes

This refund policy may be updated periodically. Members will be notified of significant changes via email. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
